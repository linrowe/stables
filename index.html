Linrowe Stable Yard

Linrowe stable yard is nestled in the heart of the equestrian hub in Kyalami AH and sports some of the most talented, dedicated and passionate equestrian coaches and competitive riders the industry has to offer. The yard offers safe and secure stabling with well maintained arenas and dedicated grooms ensuring a smooth ride throughout the year. Linrowe Showjumping Team prides themselves on staying current with the most efficient nutritional equine supplements and food sources.

The Riding School

Whether you are dreaming of horses and the life of an equestrian or competitively showjumping Linrowe is the yard that will make your dreams a reality. Linrowe Showjuming Team boasts a wealth of experienced and passionate coaches staying current with the latest on training techniques, nutrition, health and riding gear. There is no limit when riding for the Linrowe Showjuming Team, as Oscar says “Life is like the six bar. You have to stay on top of every single aspect of your discipline because they will just keep on raising the bar until somebody wins, there are no limits!”.

Riding lessons are a must if you wish to compete at show level. If you are just starting out or have been competing since you you were a toddler let our experienced coaches take the reigns and lead you to finish line.

Linrowe Stables offer quality stabling and equestrian care a trot away from the KPC show venue. An eye for detail and dedicated grooms make stabling at Linrowe a breeze with special attention payed to stabling, feeding and grooming ensures that our sport horses are in prime condition come show day.

Sales & Baits

Is owning a quality sport horse a dream of yours?

Oscar Ncube and his team use kind and gentle techniques during equestrian training, applying just the right amount of pressure to form a trusting relationship between horse and man that will carry you beyond glory.

If you are not ready to make the commitment of owning a horse of your own we offer horse baiting where you can lease a horse to practice, compete and love as your own. Full, Half and Quarter baits are available to suit your needs as an up and coming showjumper.
